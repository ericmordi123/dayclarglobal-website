About Us

Dayclar Global Ventures is a Real Estate Company in Lekki, Lagos State, Nigeria that specializes in building luxury and modern apartment for all categories of people for sale, rent and lease. We specialize in property development, project consultancy, property investment, and Facility management, provision of architectural and civil designs, and a significant proficiency in construction with modern technology.

We are a bespoke Property development, management and investment company and We strive to create high quality accommodation for our clients and to make every house a home.

Property Investment
